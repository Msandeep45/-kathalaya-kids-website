About Kathalaya Kids

At Kathalaya Kids International Pre School, we don't just teach; we transform. Our unique approach equips children with self-confidence and independence through a safe, nurturing environment. Located in Banjara Hills, we combine traditional care with modern innovation.

Why Choose Kathalaya?

📚 Story-Based Learning

We use narrative to bring concepts to life, making learning engaging and memorable.

📱 Smart Interactive Books

Proprietary technology that creates "magic" with every page turn to expand vocabulary.

🌳 Safe & Green Ambience

Beautiful roof garden and child-friendly infrastructure designed for safety and play.

👩‍🏫 Professional Staff

Caring, well-organized teachers passionate about nurturing your child's future.
